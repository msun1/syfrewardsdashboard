Overview

Welcome! This dashboard is your personalized page for all things Synchrony!

It will walk you through the different modules of our customized training under the 'My Training' tab. Based on your credit card standing, our system will generate suitable training videos and walkthroughs to ensure you make the most of your time with our products. Our goal is to be as transparent as possible to our customers.

Each time you complete the recommended trainings, you will be eligible to receive rewards, including gift cards or cash rewards through our partnering companies.

My Training

This page is dedcated for training curated specifically based on your spending habits, credit card acitivty, or other account behaviors in the past.

Completing certain tasks or a certain amount of modules menas that you will be able to earn points (listed next to each module's name), and in turn trade those points for tangible products such as gift cards to companies who are partnering with Synchrony.
